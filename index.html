<h2>🏛 Where You Are Staying — Eixample</h2>

Elegant 19th-century expansion of Barcelona with wide streets, geometric grid layout and stunning modernist architecture. 

Home of Gaudí masterpieces and one of the safest, most central areas in the city.

</section>

 

<section>

<h2>🎨 Barcelona Architecture</h2>

Barcelona is famous for Catalan Modernism — flowing shapes, colour, mosaics and nature-inspired design. 

Antoni Gaudí transformed the city into an open-air artwork.

</section>
